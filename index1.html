Our Classes

Explore our range of yoga classes suitable for all levels:

Beginner's Yoga

Focus:

  • Foundation, alignment, breathing

Poses:

  • Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
  • Downward Facing-Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)
  • Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)
  • Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana)
  • Tree Pose (Vrksasana)
  • Breathing Techniques:
  • Ujjayi (Ocean Breath)

Yoga for Kids

Focus:

  • Fun, interactive, developmental

Poses:

  • Animal Poses (Downward
  • Facing Dog, Cobra Pose)
  • Balancing Poses (Tree Pose, Eagle Pose)
  • Partner Poses (Partner Tree Pose)
  • Yoga Games (Freeze Dance, Yoga Scavenger Hunt)

Yoga for Seniors

Focus:

  • Gentle, restorative, accessible

Poses:

  • Chair Yoga (Seated Forward Fold)
  • Gentle Flow (Sun Salutations)
  • Restorative Poses (Legs Up The Wall Pose)
  • Breathing Exercises (Ujjayi, Nasal Breathing)

Benefits of Yoga

Yoga offers a variety of physical and mental benefits:

Physical Benifits

  • Flexibility and balance
  • Strengthens muscles and bones
  • Improves posture and alignment
  • Enhances circulation and cardiovascular health
  • Supports weight management
  • Improves digestion and detoxification
  • Reduces inflammation and chronic pain

Mental and Emotional Benifits

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves mood and emotional regulation
  • Supports mental clarity and cognitive function
  • Promotes relaxation and calmness
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Boosts confidence and self

Benefits for Specific Populations

  • 1. Prenatal yoga: supports healthy pregnancy and childbirth
  • Yoga for children: enhances focus, coordination, and self
  • esteem
  • Yoga for seniors: improves flexibility, balance, and cognitive function
  • Yoga for athletes: enhances performance, flexibility, and recovery
  • Yoga for individuals with disabilities: promotes accessibility and inclusivity
